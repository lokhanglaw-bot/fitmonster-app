## 3. App 描述（Description）

### 英文版

FitMonster turns your fitness journey into an exciting monster-raising adventure. Every workout you complete, every healthy meal you log, and every step you take helps your virtual monster grow stronger, happier, and evolve into powerful new forms.

**Raise Your Monster**
Hatch a unique monster egg and nurture it through your daily fitness habits. Your monster's fullness, energy, and mood are directly tied to your real-world activity — feed it by scanning your meals with AI-powered food recognition, and train it by logging your workouts.

**AI Food Scanner**
Simply point your camera at any meal and let FitMonster's AI identify the food and estimate its nutritional content — calories, protein, carbs, and fats. Your monster reacts to what you eat: balanced meals make it thrive, while junk food leaves it sluggish.

**Workout Tracking**
Choose from a variety of exercises including running, cycling, yoga, strength training, and more. A built-in timer tracks your session, and your monster earns experience points based on your effort. Sync with Apple Health to automatically import your daily steps.

**Monster Battles**
Challenge other trainers in the Battle Arena! Your monster's combat stats are powered by your real fitness data — the more active you are, the stronger your monster becomes. Find nearby trainers on the map or match with opponents worldwide.

**Social Features**
Add friends, chat with fellow trainers, and compare your monsters. Explore the map to discover nearby players and send battle challenges.

**Daily Quests & Caring System**
Complete daily quests to earn rewards. Take care of your monster by feeding it regularly and keeping it active. Neglect it, and its stats will decline — just like real fitness habits.

**Key Features:**
• AI-powered food recognition and nutrition tracking
• Workout timer with calorie and XP calculations
• Monster evolution system tied to your fitness progress
• Real-time PvP monster battles
• Apple Health integration for automatic step syncing
• Nearby player discovery with map
• Friends, chat, and social features
• Daily quests and achievement system
• Monster caring system (fullness, energy, mood)
• Light and dark mode support
• English and Chinese language support

Start your fitness adventure today — your monster is waiting!

### 中文版

在對戰競技場挑戰其他訓練師！你的怪獸戰鬥數值由真實健身數據驅動 — 你越活躍，怪獸就越強大。在地圖上尋找附近的訓練師或與全球對手配對。

**主要功能：**
• AI 食物辨識與營養追蹤
• 運動計時器與卡路里、經驗值計算
• 與健身進度連動的怪獸進化系統
• 即時 PvP 怪獸對戰
• Apple Health 整合，自動同步步數
• 地圖探索附近玩家
• 好友、聊天與社交功能
• 每日任務與成就系統
• 怪獸照顧系統（飽食度、活力值、心情）
• 淺色與深色模式
• 英文與中文語言支援

今天就開始你的健身冒險 — 你的怪獸正在等你！

## 7. 審核帳號（Review Account）

Apple 審核員需要一組帳號來測試你的 App。你需要：

1. 在你的 App 中用 Google 或 Apple 登入建立一個測試帳號
2. 在 App Store Connect 的「App Review Information」填寫：
   - **Sign-in required**: Yes
   - **Username**: （你的測試帳號 email）
   - **Password**: （你的測試帳號密碼）
   - **Notes**: "Please use Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In to log in. A test account is provided above. After login, you can test food scanning, workout tracking, monster caring, and battle features."

**注意：** 如果你的 App 使用 OAuth（Google/Apple 登入），審核員可能無法用帳號密碼登入。建議在 Notes 中說明：
> "This app uses Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In for authentication. Please use Apple Sign-In to create a test account during review."
